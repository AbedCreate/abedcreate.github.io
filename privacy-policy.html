Privacy Policy for TripGuard Last Updated: December 10, 2025 TripGuard Intel & Transit Ltd. (“we”, “our”, “us”) is committed to protecting the privacy and security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, share, and safeguard information when you use the TripGuard mobile application (the “App”). 1. Information We Collect We collect the following categories of information: 1.1 Account Information Full name Phone number Email address Profile photo Chosen role (driver or rider) 1.2 Location Data GPS coordinates used to provide caution alerts, proximity checks, and incident reporting. 1.3 Media & Reports Photos, videos, audio, documents, OCR scans, and other files you upload to support reports or safety checks. 1.4 Device & Usage Data Device identifiers Operating system information App activity logs Crash logs Diagnostics used to improve stability and performance 1.5 Communications Messages, comments, posts, and reports submitted in TripGuard forums, chats, or safety modules. 2. How We Use Your Information We use collected information to: Operate, maintain, and improve TripGuard features Verify user identity and prevent duplicate, fraudulent, or unsafe accounts Enable driver and rider safety checks Process reports, cautions, and incident submissions Provide customer support Send alerts, push notifications, and safety updates Improve user experience and safety algorithms Comply with Ghanaian law enforcement and regulatory requirements We do not use your data for automated decision-making that produces legal effects without human involvement. 3. Sharing of Information 3.1 Service Providers We share information with trusted vendors who help operate TripGuard, including: Firebase Authentication Firestore Database Google Cloud Analytics and crash-reporting tools These providers process data strictly on our behalf. 3.2 Community Members Information you intentionally submit in: Public forums Comments Reports Incident descriptions …may be visible to other verified TripGuard users. TripGuard does not publicly display your identity; only the text you submit is shown. 3.3 Legal Authorities We may disclose data: When required by Ghanaian law During criminal investigations To prevent fraud, harm, or threats to public safety 3.4 No Sale of Personal Data We do not sell your personal information. 4. Data Security We apply multiple safeguards, including: Firebase Authentication security Firestore access rules Data encryption in transit Access controls and audit logging While we take reasonable measures to protect your data, no digital platform is entirely risk-free. 5. Data Retention Account information: kept while your account is active Reports & evidence: retained for safety, dispute resolution, and legal compliance Log data: retained for diagnostics and security auditing You may request deletion of your data at any time. 6.
