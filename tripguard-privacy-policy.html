Privacy Policy for TripGuard

Last Updated: December 10, 2025

TripGuard Intel & Transit Ltd. (“we”, “our”, “us”) is committed to protecting the privacy and security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, share, and safeguard information when you use the TripGuard mobile application (“App”).

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following categories of information:

1.1 Account Information

  • Full name
  • Phone number
  • Email address
  • Profile photo
  • Chosen role (driver or rider)

1.2 Location Data

GPS coordinates used to provide caution alerts, proximity checks, and incident reporting.

1.3 Media & Reports

Photos, videos, audio, documents, OCR scans, and files uploaded to support reports or safety checks.

1.4 Device & Usage Data

  • Device identifiers
  • Operating system information
  • App activity logs
  • Crash logs
  • Diagnostics for stability and performance

1.5 Communications

Messages, comments, posts, and reports submitted in TripGuard forums, chats, or safety modules.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Operate, maintain, and improve TripGuard
  • Verify user identity and prevent unsafe accounts
  • Enable driver and rider safety checks
  • Process reports, cautions, and incident submissions
  • Provide customer support
  • Send push notifications and safety alerts
  • Enhance safety algorithms
  • Comply with Ghanaian law enforcement requirements

3. Sharing of Information

3.1 Service Providers

We share data with trusted vendors including Firebase Authentication, Firestore, Google Cloud, analytics, and crash-reporting tools.

3.2 Community Members

Information intentionally submitted in public forums or safety modules may be visible to other verified TripGuard users. Your identity is never publicly displayed.

3.3 Legal Authorities

Data may be disclosed when required by Ghanaian law, during investigations, or to prevent threats to public safety.

3.4 No Sale of Data

We do not sell your personal information.

4. Data Security

We apply safeguards including encryption, access controls, audit logging, and Firebase security rules.

5. Data Retention

  • Account info: kept while your account is active
  • Reports & evidence: retained for safety and legal compliance
  • Logs: retained for diagnostics

6. Children’s Privacy

TripGuard is not intended for users under 18. Accounts of minors may be suspended or deleted.

7. Your Rights

  • Access your data
  • Update or correct your information
  • Request account deletion
  • Request a data copy
  • Opt out of non-essential notifications

To exercise these rights, contact: tripguardapp@gmail.com

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy. Continued use of TripGuard after changes indicates acceptance.
